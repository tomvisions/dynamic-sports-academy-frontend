DYNAMIC SPORTS ACADEMY.

Dynamic Sports Academy is a sports club located at Agona Asafo, close to Agona Swedru in the central region of Ghana. The Academy was founded by Mr. George Papamikidis, who is the President and CEO of the institution.

Registered and Established in July 2010, primarily as the Asafo United Football club playing and operating in the central region 2nd division league.

After several seasons of success in the 2nd division league, In 2015, the operational focus shifted to unearthing and developing juvenile talents in the Ghana and Africa as the Cadima Sports Academy with the operating name Asafo Cadima.

Currently operating as the Dynamic Academy, the team works with talented young players scouted from all over Ghana and Africa in the following youth categories.

  • Boys Under 13
  • Boys Under 15
  • Boys Under 17

Affiliated to the Ghana Football Association in 2010, Our juvenile teams participates in the Central regional and district juvenile colts league and the Agona Distrcit Juvenile Football League.

Dynamic Academy currently also operates an amateur mens team comprising of players over 17 years and semi-professional players preparing to make the big step into fulltime professional sports in Ghana and the international markets.
